G String

The G string is the third string, directly above the B string.


  • The lowest G on the staff is played open.

  • The A is played by holding a finger on the second fret.

  • The B is played by holding a finger on the fourth fret.

  • The C is played by holding a finger on the fifth fret.

  • The D is played by holding a finger on the seventh fret.

  • The E is played by holding a finger on the ninth fret.

  • The F is played by holding a finger on the tenth fret.

  • The G is played by holding a finger on the twelfth fret.
