e string

Intentionally lowercase,this is the first string to learn. Some may find it weird, but when learning guitar, you learn from the bottom up.

The e string plays the highest notes. The notes shown below are all played on the e string.


  • The E still within the staff is played open.

  • The F is played by holding a finger on the first fret.

  • The G is played by holding a finger on the third fret.

  • The A is played by holding a finger on the fifth fret.

  • The B is played by holding a finger on the seventh fret.

  • The C is played by holding a finger on the eight fret.

  • The D is played by holding a finger on the tenth fret.

  • The E is played by holding a finger on the twelfth fret.
